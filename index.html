Who We Are

A Student-Led Mission of Compassion

The Dominican Republic Outreach Program (DROP) is a student organization at Florida Gulf Coast University (FGCU) committed to community volunteering in the Santiago area of the Dominican Republic.

We serve some of the most vulnerable children in the region, kids living in extreme poverty, homeless youth, and children from both the Dominican Republic and Haiti who are forced to work on the streets simply to survive.

"These children deserve more than survival, they deserve a childhood. Your support makes that possible."

Every dollar donated goes directly toward food, supplies, and community programs for the children we serve. We are entirely community-funded and run by passionate student volunteers.

Our Work

How We Help

On the ground in Santiago, our volunteers deliver direct support to children who need it most.

🍽️

Food & Nutrition

We raise monetary donations that go directly toward purchasing food for the children we serve, ensuring they have the nourishment they need to learn and grow.

🎒

Essential Supplies

Distributing clothing, hygiene items, and school materials to children who lack access to basic necessities.

📚

Education & Play

Each year we carefully prepare special educational lessons and activities in advance, ensuring every child we serve receives meaningful learning experiences and dedicated support for their growth.

🤝

Community Outreach

We partner with Acción Callejera in Santiago to reach Dominican and Haitian children in the most underserved areas.
